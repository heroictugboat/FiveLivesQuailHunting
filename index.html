About Five-Live's

Welcome to the newest quail huntinng paradise in your neighborhood. Built on friendly atmosphere, good service and the beautiful outdoors. The 5 Live's Ranch has dedicates nearly five hundred acres to quail habit. With native bunch grass, brushy fence lines and plenty of water which all adds up to extraoridinary quail hunting adventure. If you dream of grassy fields, pointing dogs and coveys of quail exploding into the blue shy, then remember Barkers Birds and Buds. Whether entertaining clients or just spending the day with family or your buddies, we are excited to have you spend the day with us.

Daily Fees

  • Limit 12 quail per hunter $145 per hunter.
  • Birds over the limit $11 each
  • Guide fees $40 per hunter for 1/2 day (mininum of ? hunters)
  • Hunters with there own dogs are welcomed
  • PLEASE MAKE RESERVATIONS at least 5 days prior to the Hunt
  • Checks or Cash (No Credit Cards Accepted)
  • 50% (non-refundable) deposit required with reservation, do honor rain checks
  • *Lodging Accommodations not available at this time*
  • *Corporate Packages are available upon request*

Requirements

  • License Requirements
  • Texas Residents require a valid Texas Hunting License
  • Out of state hunters are required to have a special license
  • Safety Requirements
  • Wear gun saftey approved shooting glasses
  • Fluorescent orange cap or vest
  • Inform our staff of inexperienced hunters
  • Young hunters must have shooting experience and have taken a hunters saftey class
